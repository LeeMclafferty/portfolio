Gameplay Programmer

I am a gameplay programmer and software enthusiast with the life long goal to never stop learning. My greatest strength is my work ethic, which is fueled by my undeniable drive to improve in everything that I do. I have spent the last two years using online resources to teach myself how to program, and have now translated that skill into creating my games. While Programming gameplay is a passion of mine, I have also branched out into other parts of game development to understand the entire process. Some of the other game development areas that I have explored include but are not limited to: shaders, concept art, and asset modeling. The combination of my current knowledge and my professional background in server scripting and management puts me in a unique position as I possess both technical skills and the communication skills required to effectively work with a team.
